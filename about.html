About Our Company

Everfirst Engineering and Automation desires to attain full potential to meet the Power Energy needs for Industries as well as Domestic requirements provided by Public Power Plants in the country. Our mission is to achieve the maximum level of Power generation at competitive & reasonable rates with a view to Industrial Productivity and commencement in other areas. To execute the task we have a well-built service team comprising dedicated, skilled and foreign-trained Engineers. EVERFIRST is a service oriented company who is involved in sales and after sales services of sub-station and power generation.

What we do

EEA offers extensive Engineering Service and maintenance facilities to provide better customer service of Supply, Instal­lation, testing & commissioning of;

  • Electrical Sub- Station
  • Generator
  • Busbar-Trunking System IEC/ KEMA/DEKRA (STL) Standard

We also provide:

  • Low, Medium and High Voltage (11kV and above) IEC standard switchgear panels for control, protection and metering from overseas and locally fabricated.
  • Distribution of complete range of IEC/KEMA standards power transformers.
  • Electrical consultancy and customized Sync system for all kinds of Power Generation Plant and Grid paralleling by using the most reliable controllers such as easYgen(Woodward)-3000 series and DSE 8000 series controllers
